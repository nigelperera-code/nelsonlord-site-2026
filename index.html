What we look for

Good businesses with steady demand.

We are open-minded on sector. What matters is the shape of the business. Repeat or recurring revenue, predictable demand, a reputation built over time, and a team that can keep things running as an owner steps back.

If a business depends on constant marketing, rapid growth, or one individual holding everything together, it is usually not the right fit for us.

How we operate

We take responsibility for running businesses properly.

Most established businesses already work. Our job is not to reinvent them. Our job is to steady them.

We focus on clear priorities, clear responsibilities, and calm leadership. We support managers and teams so decisions do not sit with one person.

  • A simple weekly rhythm so priorities stay clear
  • Clear visibility on workload, cash, and delivery
  • Support for managers through a transition
  • Consistent communication with key customers
  • Practical improvements that compound over time
What happens after a sale

In most cases, not much changes.

We aim to protect what is already working, look after people, and strengthen the foundations.

  • Keep the team in place
  • Look after long-standing customers
  • Reduce reliance on one person over time
  • Add structure and visibility where it genuinely helps

If it is not right, you will know quickly. If it is, we will agree next steps calmly.
