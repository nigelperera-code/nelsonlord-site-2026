The intelligence gap in business acquisition.

Most acquirers rely on brokers, databases, and public information to find deals. The result is that they are always competing for the same opportunities, at the same inflated prices, with the same information as everyone else.

The best deals — the ones where you acquire a genuinely excellent business at a fair price — come from proprietary intelligence. From people who know the sector, know the players, and can identify the businesses that are ready to transition before they ever reach the open market.

"The best off-market deals come from people who are not in the business of selling businesses, but who are trusted by business owners at the moment they are most receptive to a conversation about their future."

The sectors we are building in.

We focus on two distinct lanes, both characterised by recurring revenue, regulatory drivers, and fragmented ownership structures that create platform-building opportunities.

  • Lane 1 — B2B Compliance & Regulatory Services. Water hygiene, environmental testing, fire compliance, legionella control, and similar services where demand is mandated by regulation and customers are locked in by necessity.
  • Lane 2 — Clinically Governed Healthcare. ADHD assessment clinics, private mental health services, and other regulated healthcare businesses with recurring prescription or monitoring revenue and NHS spillover demand.
  • Adjacent Opportunities. Any established UK service business with £150k+ EBITDA, recurring revenue, and a fragmented competitive landscape where we can build a platform over time.
