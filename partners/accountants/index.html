A simple, respectful process.

We have designed our engagement process to be as easy as possible for you and your client. There is no pressure, no obligation, and no disruption to your client relationship.

  • A quiet conversation with us. You share a brief, anonymous description of the business. We confirm whether it fits our criteria within 48 hours.
  • An introduction, on your terms. If there is a potential fit, you introduce us to your client at a time and in a manner that feels right for them. We take it from there.
  • A no-obligation valuation. We provide your client with a clear, honest picture of what a structured, private sale could look like, with no pressure to proceed.
  • A structured, tax-efficient deal. We work alongside you to structure the transaction in a way that meets your client's personal financial goals, often using deferred consideration or vendor loan notes.
  • A clean close. We have committed capital. We do not re-trade. When we issue a Letter of Intent, we intend to complete.

The profile of a Nelson Lord acquisition.

We are disciplined acquirers. We are not interested in every business; we are interested in the right businesses. This focus means that when we say a business is a fit, it genuinely is.

  • £150,000 – £500,000+ in sustainable EBITDA
  • Recurring or contracted revenue - compliance services, testing, B2B subscriptions, regulated services
  • A fragmented market where we can build a platform over time
  • A founder who is genuinely motivated to exit over a 6–24 month transition
  • A team that can run the business without the founder being present every day

We actively avoid project-based businesses, heavy manufacturing, and businesses where the founder is the sole source of revenue or relationships.
