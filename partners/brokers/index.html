Our acquisition mandate.

We are disciplined acquirers. We know exactly what we are looking for, which means we can give you a fast, accurate assessment of any opportunity you bring us.

Before you send us a full information memorandum, run through this quick checklist. If you can answer yes to most of these, it is worth a conversation.

  • 1
    Is sustainable EBITDA £150,000 or above? We are not the right buyer for micro-businesses.
  • 2
    Is at least 50% of revenue recurring or contracted? Predictable revenue is our primary filter.
  • 3
    Is the founder willing to stay for a transition period? We value continuity and a smooth handover.
  • 4
    Is no single customer more than 30% of revenue? We avoid binary concentration risk.
  • 5
    Is there a clear, genuine reason for the sale? Retirement, health, or succession — not just testing the market.
  • 6
    Is the business not dependent on heavy paid advertising? We prefer organic, embedded demand drivers.

The sectors we are actively building in.

  • Established B2B Service Businesses — any sector with recurring revenue, defensible market position, and £150k+ EBITDA
